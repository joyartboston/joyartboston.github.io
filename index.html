JoyArt Boston 2024

JoyArt is an intercollegiate game art competition for connecting students with industry professionals! Anyone who is currently a student (or was within the past 6 months) in the Boston area is encouraged to submit - you don't need to make something new, and are welcome to submit existing portfolio pieces. There will be a prize for each category winner.

Learn More

Share your Joy of Game Art!

Submissions close January 26th. Finalists will be announced within a few days and will be invited to attend the in-person event on February 9th.

The finalist event for JoyArt 2024 will be hosted at WPI’s Seaport location in Boston, MA (303 Congress Street), from 5:00-8:00pm on February 9th. Check back in later to reserve tickets!
Finalists must be in attendance to be considered for final judging.

Full details about the event, prize categories, and judging criteria can be found in our JoyArt 2024 Details document.
